# Daphstar Fitness App - Product Brief

## Overview
A simple booking system for Daphstar Fitness that lets customers view a published class schedule, book a spot, and cancel when needed. The goal is to reduce manual scheduling while keeping the experience easy for clients and simple to manage for the business owner.

## Goals
- Let customers book and cancel classes quickly from a phone.
- Provide the owner a simple way to publish and adjust class schedules.
- Reduce no-shows with clear policies and confirmations.
- Launch fast with a minimal, reliable feature set.

## Target Users
- Customers who attend fitness classes and book on their phone.
- Business owner (admin) who publishes classes and manages capacity.

## MVP Scope
### Customer features
- View class schedule (date, time, class type, spots left).
- Book a class.
- Cancel a booking.
- Receive confirmation by email.

### Admin features
- Create and edit class schedule.
- Set capacity per class.
- See attendee list per class.
- Cancel a class and notify attendees.

## Out of Scope (Later)
- Payments and memberships.
- Waitlists.
- SMS notifications.
- Multi-location support.
- Instructor management.

## Success Metrics
- 80%+ of bookings completed without owner assistance.
- Reduction in manual scheduling messages.
- Fewer than 2 booking-related issues per week after launch.

## Assumptions
- Initial launch can be a mobile-friendly web app.
- Email is the primary notification channel.
- One location and one business owner admin.

## Open Questions
- What is the cancellation window and no-show policy?
- Should customers create accounts or use one-time booking links?
- Do you want public visibility of class capacity?
- Do you want a public landing page and marketing info?
